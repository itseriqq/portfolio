To-do App.

Useful.

The project was developed to work in any device, with simple functions to make it useful to everyone that would like to take notes of their daily obligations.

Editable.

Categories.

The user can divide his items by a color pallete. This procedure makes it easy to separate between categories. And select easily the items he would like to check, edit or delete.

Fast.

User-friendly.

The way it's editable makes it even more minimalist. The visual elements are organized in a clear way, and the user can set colors of its preference, and also setting the category of each card. This allows visitors to focus on what really matters:

take notes.

Attractive.

Interactions.

I chose to do not display interactions until user chooses to interact by clicking on the elements on the webapp, offering users intuitive and efficient navigation. This approach simplifies visual to website information and functionality, providing a more pleasant and user-friendly experience.
